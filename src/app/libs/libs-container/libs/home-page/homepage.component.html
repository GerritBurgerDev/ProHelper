Our Services

We serve as a platform container, blog site, informational website and we are here to help you with some of that heavy lifting!

Our Blogs service allows users to communicate with other users with regards to a wide variety of Pokemon related topics such as: Games, Anime Series, Pokemon In General and much more!. See what other people are talking about, get in touch today.

Platform creators of all kinds can contact us to serve as an informational hub to their projects OR even serve as a helper for their users. Given that they have an API we can offer all kinds of cool services for their users!

We also serve as an informational hub for Pokemon in general or other platforms. We can give you all kinds of cool information with just the click of a button!

Blogging

We also serve as a blogging platform for our users!

Get connected with other Pokemon lovers on a variety of topics. Users can share useful information they found on our web application or anywhere else or just join the banter and have a good conversation among friends.

Our FAQ

Confused? Check out our FAQ page to find answers for frequently asked questions.

The FAQ page only contains the most frequently asked questions for specific platforms or categories, however, we added the functionality of being about to ask us a question directly! Pop us a message or even try asking some questions on our blog!

Donations

Okay, okay, yes, this is were we ask for some money. At the moment ProHelper is fully run on donations by the public and we need your help to stay afloat!

At the moment we can only accept PayPal donations, however, we plan to accept CryptoCurrency donations soon.

We do not plan to stay donation funded forever. Otherwise we won't be able to keep up with the costs of running this service. Hence, we plan to bring subscriptions to the web application, users who have donated will not lose any privileges they had while we were donation funded as a THANK YOU!, to them. Platforms added to ProHelper will also be required to pay a subscription and startup fee (please see our services for more information).
